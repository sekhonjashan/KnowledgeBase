ARAS

A running prototype for Annotated Relational Algebra System (or ARAS, for short), that implements the work of Green et al [PODS 2007][1] on provenance semirings. Implementing and extending to Databases with different annotated views as mentioned below : (1) Bag semantics (2) Probability (3) Certainty (4) Provenance polynomial (5) Standard Case Query answering in these semantics involves generalizing the relational algebra (RA+) to perform corresponding operations on the annotations [1]
